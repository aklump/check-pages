Dates

The YAML parser will convert unquoted strings which look like a date or a date-time into a Unix timestamp. To avoid this you must quote your dates as shown below.

-
  my_custom_mixin:
    -
      d: '2023-03-09'
      cd: 8
      or: 100
      cf: soft
