Sleep: Adding a Delay

sleep: <seconds> provides a means of adding a delay to the test runner.

Example Tests

-
  visit: /test_subject.php
  find:
    -
      header: X-timestamp
      set: time

-
  why: Wait three seconds to ensure REQUEST_TIME increases.
  sleep: 3

-
  visit: /test_subject.php
  find:
    -
      header: X-timestamp
      set: time2
    -
      eval: ${time2} >= ${time} + 3

Provided by the sleep handler.
